 we use length in width,height,margin,padding,fontsize 

there are two types of units in css.........
1)Absolute
2)Reletive 

1)absolute unit--------> 

Cm,Mm,Inch,Px(1 inch=96px),Pt(1 inch=72pt),Pc(1pc=12pt) 

2)Reletive unit-------->

Em,Rem,Vh,Vw,%


** Em is reletive to the size of its direct parent
** Rm is only reletive to the root size(html tag)
** Vh is 1% of view port height 
** vW is 1% of view port width 
** % reletive to the parent
